About the project

Why this simulator matters

WritersHub Kenya is designed for classroom learning and community outreach. It helps learners identify the classic patterns of online writing scams, including advance-fee demands, fake assignment pages, and manipulation of trust.

Victim View

Realistic writing job listings that hide the scam behind an activation payment and restricted bid process.

Admin Dashboard

Protected admin tools for publishing fake jobs, viewing activation revenue, and seeing which members have been blocked or activated.

Awareness Focus

Built to teach the warning signs of online Ponzi schemes and improve critical thinking when reviewing freelance offers.

Functionality

How the simulation works

1. Public job board

Visitors see polished writing assignments and are invited to bid for work.

2. Activation barrier

Bids are blocked until the user pays the KSh 500 activation fee, exposing the scam dynamic.

3. Admin controls

Educators post fake jobs, monitor payments, and review member activity through a dashboard.

Get started

  • Start the frontend and backend servers
